about Next Gears

Next Gears™ is the world’s leading lifestyle brand for gamers.

the triple-headed snake trademark of Next Gears is one of the most recognized logos in the global gaming and esports communities. with a fan base that spans every continent, the company has designed and built the world’s largest gamer-focused ecosystem of hardware, software and services.

next gears’s award-winning hardware includes high-performance gaming peripherals and Blade gaming laptops. next nears’s software platform, with over 175 million users, includes next gears Synapse (an Internet of Things platform), next gears Chroma rgb (a proprietary rgb lighting technology system supporting thousands of devices and hundreds of games/apps), and next gears Cortex (a game optimizer and launcher). next gears also offers payment services for gamers, youth, millennials and generation z. next gears gold is one of the world’s largest game payment services, and next gears fintech provides fintech services in emerging markets. founded in 2005, next gears is dual headquartered in egypt (nasercity) and elmonfey with regional headquarters in elmaady and saqr qresh. next gears has 19 offices worldwide and is recognized as the leading brand for gamers in the usa, europe and china.
